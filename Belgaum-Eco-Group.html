#SaveTheWesternGhats

The Ministry of Environment, Forest, and Climate Change (MoEF&CC) has released its 6th draft notification on the conservation of the Western Ghats.

Belgaum Eco Group has been working on ground to get the pulse of the citizens about the ESA notification. The citizens of Khanapur taluka and it's adjacent areas in Maharashtra, Goa, Uttara Kannada, Dharwad -Hubli districts and others want their areas to be notified as ESA and get the required protection from damaging activities like Mining, Quarrying etc. We feel its high time Western Ghats gets the required protection NOW

We’re calling on all concerned citizens to communicate this message to the MOEF so that these areas get notifed as ESA NOW and stop further damage of the Western Ghats
